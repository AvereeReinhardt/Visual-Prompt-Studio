**Prompt** Create a highly realistic oil painting on canvas in the style of early 20th-century Surrealism. The composition is simple and centered: a single tobacco pipe floats against a smooth, warm beige background. The stem curves gently upward and to the right in a long, elegant arc, becoming thinner toward the mouthpiece. Use subtle directional light from the upper left, creating gradual value transitions and a mild chiaroscuro effect. Highlights should appear along the upper left edge of the bowl and stem, while deeper browns and near-black tones define the underside, giving the illusion of volume and mass.
